Sanctuary Rabbits

Our Sanctuary Rabbits are special rabbits who are no longer likely to be adopted but remain in the Indiana House Rabbit Society's care. They are provided endless love, long-term care and support.

Akua

Breed: Dwarf Mix

Sex: Female

Age: 8 Months

Weight: 4 Pounds

I'm Akua and I'm new here! I was found running loose for at least a month, it was cold out and hard to find food! I'm happy to be a house rabbit now.

I am a very sweet bunny and very curious. I love my run time! My foster family says I can be shy at first and may take a little more time warming up to a person. Once I warm up, I want attention and enjoy being petted!
