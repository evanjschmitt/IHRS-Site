Coco

Breed: Mix

Sex: Female

Age: 2 Years, 11 Months

Weight: 5 Pounds

Bio

Coco and four other rabbits were found wondering around a neighborhood in Merrillville, IN. Since is grateful to be a house rabbit and settled in quickly.

Coco is a very curious, social with both people and other rabbits, and loves to be petted. Her play area is full of toys and Coco bunstructed a custom made playhouse from a cardboard box. She is a very busy girl!

Coco would do well in a home where she has alot of space to run around and explore as she is full of energy.
