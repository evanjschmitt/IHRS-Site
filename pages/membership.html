IHRS Membership

You can voice your ongoing support for IHRS by becoming a member. Benefits of membership include:

  • Membership to House Rabbit Society ($20 value + discounts)
  • 1 Free Nail Trim for your bunny (must be able to bring your bunny to Indianapolis -- $10 value)
  • Small IHRS themed gift
  • Members only newsletter
  • Members only events

Please complete the membership request form below. If this is a gift for someone, please complete the form with the member's information.
